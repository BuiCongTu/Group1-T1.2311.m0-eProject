Spa

What is spa?

What is a spa? The word "spa" was born in the 19th and early 20th centuries. It means a resort centered around a stream - using water to treat diseases and help the spirit feel more refreshed. As the years passed and demand increased. Today's spa is a place of therapeutic care that encourages change, bringing absolute comfort to the mind, body and spirit.

often confuse spas with the wellness industry. Because these two industries both have the purpose of improving the quality of human health. So how is it different?

  1. The wellness center includes a range of services that are based on medical therapies while the spa is ideal for relaxation;
  2. The wellness services offered are mainly geared towards various forms of massage, while the spa offers services mainly geared towards cosmetic treatments and body care;
  3. In some cases, wellness is defined as a necessity while spa is defined as a luxury. Therefore, the price of health care services is often lower than the price of spa services.
  4. Find out how much it costs to open a small spa so you can prepare carefully before doing business.

Types of spa models and what spa services include

Mineral spring spa

These spas offer on-site natural mineral, thermal or seawater resources used in hydrotherapy treatments. This is considered the origin method of the original spa.

Medical spa

A medical spa is a combination of a day spa and a medical clinic operated under the close supervision of medical doctors. The most common services offered at a medical spa are IPL (intense pulsed light) treatments, laser hair removal, laser treatments, skin rejuvenation, etc.
