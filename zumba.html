ZUMBA DANCE FITNESS

Zumba Dance Fitness is a discipline created by talented Colombian dancer Alberto 'Beto' Pérez in the 1990s. The discipline originated from South America, the birthplace of world-famous dances. With dance movements that combine simple, easy-to-memorize, and easy-to-learn gymnastic movements with vibrant Latin music, Zumba has become a very popular subject.
