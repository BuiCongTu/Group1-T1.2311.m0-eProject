Sexy Dance

Sexy Dance is a dance discipline originated from the 'jazz funk' style of Latin America. This is a fusion of Hiphop and jazz Dance. With modern, youthful, and charming dance moves, Sexy Dance highlights the body lines. The dances are often combined with lively Latin music, making Sexy Dance a very popular discipline.
